Welcome to Snapshot!

Snapshot gives you a quick glance at what is happening today - weather, news, and local events - in one compact package.

  • Weather and events are generated based upon your location or by entering a new location.
  • Today's top stories from news, sports, entertainment, and financial publications* are automatically generated; you can also enter your own search term for each article feed.
  • Only the top of each feed is displayed initially; if you want to scroll through all of the results, click on the "Scroll..." button to unlock scrolling of those results.
  • When you're ready to proceed, click the Snap button below!

*News publications: The Washington Post, Associated Press, The New York Times, Al Jazeera, BBC News, Politico, and The Economist.
Sports publications: Bleacher Report, Talksport, The Sport Bible, NFL News, and NHL News.
Entertainment publications: Entertainment Weekly and MTV News.
Financial publications: Financial Post, Financial Times, Fortune, and Business Insider.
NOTE: Articles are from the publications themselves AND their affiliated bloggers.

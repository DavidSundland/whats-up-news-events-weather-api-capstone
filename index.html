Welcome to Snapshot!

Snapshot gives you a quick glance at what is happening today - weather, news, and local events - in one compact package. Snapshot:

  • Determines your location to retrieve weather and events information.
  • If your browser doesn't support geolocation, you decline geolocation, or you want to retrieve information for a different area, you can manually enter a location.
  • Today's top stories from news, sports, entertainment, and financial publications are displayed, sorted initially by popularity.
  • You can enter your own search term for each article feed.
  • Only the top stories are initially displayed; if you want to see more, click on the "Scroll..." button to scroll through all of the articles on each topic.
  • When you're ready to proceed, click Snap!
